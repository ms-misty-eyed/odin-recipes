A tender and tasty salmon

I used to dislike salmon, but this recepie made me love it again.

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon filet
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of ginger
  • 2 tbsp of maple syrup (or honey)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, place the salmon filet. Poke some holes into the filet with a fork to let the salmon absorb the marinade.
  2. Pour the soy sauce and the maple syrup on the salmon.
  3. With a spoon, spread the ginger on the salmon.
  4. After 10-20 minutes, flip the salmon.
  5. After another 10-20 minutes, bake at 400ºF for 20 to 30 minutes, depending on your cooking preferences.
