The perfect marinated chicken for every occasion

This recepie can be used for every occasion, whether with pasta, noodles, rice, and even a salad. It is possible to marinate the chicken for a longer time, but 40 minutes is enough to get a good taste if you are in a rush.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 2 tbsp of maple syrup (or honey)
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika powder
  • 2 tbsp of Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix every ingredient. Add the chicken thighs and marinate for 40 minutes.
  2. Put the chicken in the oven at 400ºF for 20 minutes. For a crispier result, you can grill it on a pan or on the bbq
